order: 10
---
The board of directors list is a list of board members. Each board member has the following fields:

* **NAME**\
    The full name of the board member.

* **TITLE**\
    The board member's title.

<Aside>
    You can add the board of directors list to a page using the [`<BoardOfDirectors />` short code](/gently-hugged/editing-content/short-codes/board-of-directors/). 
</Aside>
